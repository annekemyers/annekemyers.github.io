​CONTENT - CUSTOM CVs

(proofreading, rewriting, layout, structure, typography)
Having trouble with your CV? No surprises there. It can all feel so personal, and yet so impersonal at the same time. You can spend many hours lost in your own content, infuriated by formatting issues, and sick and tired of the whole thing. And you can still end up with something you don't really like.

HOW I CAN HELP

I can bring a fresh perspective to your CV and to the story it tells about you. With my combination of analytical, language and design skills, I can help to make your CV something you can be proud of. 
need a quote?

WHAT I DO

I offer a custom service which treats each CV on its merits.
I do not use templates or other quick fixes.
This is because your CV should be tailored to your content.

 I will not only work on those pesky formatting issues – layout, design and typography – but also find a logical solution to the question of what goes where. I will also work with you to identify your best content, and make sure it is presented in the best way.

For some of my clients, we have rebuilt a CV from scratch, either to reflect a change in career direction, or for a return to work following time out of the paid workforce. This has worked really well, and helped them to get the jobs they wanted!

Read what my clients say about my work on their CVs and responses to selection criteria.

WHY AN EDITOR IS PERFECT FOR THIS WORK

Working on a CV brings together many of my skills as a professional editor:
  • taking a global view of the document
  • considering its purpose and readership
  • assessing its organisation, internal logic, content, layout and style
followed by one or more of the following:
  • structural editing (improving flow, structure and layout).
  • style editing. formatting and typography
  • copyediting
  • proofreading

WHAT I DON'T DO

I do not write claims against selection criteria on your behalf.
My services are editing, proofreading, typography and content strategy.
In other words, I work with content that you have already written.﻿
