content > proofreading your website

Do you have a website for your business? Do you need a helping hand with improving the quality of your content? If so, proofreading for your website might be a good start.
¶
I will review your content, and go over your entire website a number of times. I will proofread every word, and check your spelling, grammar, expression, and punctuation. If you want me to, I can also look at updating your content and/or testing for usability.
¶
Improving these things will improve the quality of your website. It will mean that you make a better first impression online. And people will stay longer on your website, reading more of your content—which is the whole point, really.
